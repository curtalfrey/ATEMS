Professional Inventory Control Software for Technicians

  • Automated Accountability:
    • Streamlined email reminders are seamlessly integrated to prompt customers for timely check-ins, ensuring optimal availability for concurrent tasks.
    • Automated report generation and email notifications preemptively address tool and equipment calibration, averting potential delays in job execution.
  • Comprehensive Tracking:
    • Utilize active/passive RFID barcode labels with scan-gun or tablet functionality to monitor:
    • Personnel responsible for checking in/out items
    • Timestamps of item check-in/out activities
    • Locations of item transactions
    • Job associations for utilized items
    • Duration of item checkouts
    • Condition and calibration status at check in/out
  • Efficient Equipment Management:
    • Record all transactions for streamlined auditing processes.
    • Document serviceability data for both calibrated and non-calibrated electronic test equipment.
    • Track purchase and calibration costs for comprehensive financial oversight.
    • Maintain records of calibration procedures, standards, and certificates used by technicians/vendors to ensure compliance with NIST standards.
  • Effortless Inventory Management:
    • Centralize control of inventory through a single, user-friendly program.
    • Manage customer and supervisor information seamlessly for enhanced organizational efficiency.
  • Simplified Reporting:
    • Easily generate, filter, and sort reports according to specific needs.
    • Access pre-generated or customize reports for tailored insights.
    • Export reports to PDF or Excel formats for convenient sharing and archival.
    • Choose between print or email options for flexible dissemination.
